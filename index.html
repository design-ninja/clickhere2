We Are Hiring

Clickhere LTD is looking for people who are passionate about crafting phenomenal web and mobile applications.

As a Frontend Engineer you’ll use your extensive knowledge of JavaScript, HTML, and CSS to improve the Slack web client, a complex in-browser application relied upon by millions of users every day. You will work with real-time data streams, engineer for performance across browsers, and delight people by making the best software we can imagine.

You will collaborate closely with Product, Design, Application Engineering, and QA to spec, conceptualize, build, test and deploy new features for our large (and growing!) user base.

As Devops Engineer you will be responsible for managing and automating the company's software delivery pipeline, ensuring the availability and reliability of the production environment, and collaborating with development teams to improve the speed and quality of our software releases.

You will build and maintain the company's infrastructure on cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, or GCP. Your daily tasks will include automation of the infrastructure deployment and configuration management using tools like Terraform, Ansible, or Puppet as well as managing and optimizing the usage and cost of cloud resources.
